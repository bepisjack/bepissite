Have an idea for a new Bepisopoly card?
Submit your best ideas below, and they might make it into the real game!

be descriptive!!
be specific about a card's functions; for an item card, when can it be used? for a movement card, does it activate the destination space? if your card's effect has a condition like owning a monopoly, what happens if you don't own one?
be creative!!
bepisopoly has lots of complex cards, so the sky's the limit when it comes to just how unique your card can be!
be considerate!!
think about if the card meaningfully impacts the game while being fun for all players; how would you feel drawing it, or how would you feel if your opponent drew it?
